# The Power of Nothing

Whitespace, or negative space, is arguably the most critical element of modern design. It's not just "empty" space; it's the breathing room that allows your content to speak.

## Typography as UI

In minimalist design, typography often takes center stage. Choosing the right font pairing and establishing a clear hierarchy can do the heavy lifting that complex graphics usually do.

*   **Headings**: Keep them bold and readable.
*   **Body Text**: Optimize for legibility. Line height and letter spacing matter.

## Micro-Interactions

Minimalism doesn't mean boring. Subtle micro-interactions—a soft gradient shift on hover, a smooth transform when a card is clicked—breathe life into a minimalist interface without cluttering it.

> "Good design is as little design as possible." - Dieter Rams
